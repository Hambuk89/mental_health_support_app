SELF HELP TOOLS

EXERCISE 1 — BREATHING

1. Inhale slowly for 4 seconds. Let your belly rise gently.

2. Hold for 2 seconds. Keep your shoulders relaxed.

3. Exhale for 6 seconds. Release the air slowly and fully.

Repeat this cycle 5 times to calm your body and settle your mind.

EXERCISE 2 — MEDITATION

1. Sit comfortably. Rest your hands on your lap and soften your gaze.

2. Notice your breath. Let it flow naturally without trying to change it.

3. Return gently. If your mind wanders, bring it back to your breath with kindness.

Try this for 1–2 minutes to create a sense of grounding and presence.
